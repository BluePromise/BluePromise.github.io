Sigmas Against Domestic Abuse

Each year the men of Phi Beta Sigma Fraternity Incorporated seek to raise awareness of the serious issue that domestic violence is in out communities. Phi Beta Sigma organizes fundraisers that educate men on the importance of never laying a hand on a woman, providing resources for help in case you or someone you know is a victim of domestic violence as well as making monetary contributions that specialize in the area of domestic violence prevention and aid.

This year on The Gamma Lambda Sigma Chapter of Phi Beta Sigma will be raising awareness and procedes for victims of Domestic Violence by partnering with Painting with a Twist.

Procedes will go to benefit the Crisis Center North who is committed to assiting vicitms of domestic violence in our community. The Center provides free empowerment counseling and advocacy to adult, teen, and child victims of domestic violence. Services are provided to individuals regardless of race, age, gender, disability, economic status, or sexual orientation.
