How We Got Started

Blue Promise was created by the brothers of the Gamma Lambda Sigma Alumni Chapter of Phi Beta Sigma Fraternity, Inc., a professional civic and service organization, in September of 2014.

Blue Promise promotes education and social programs targeting young males between the ages of 8 - 18 designed to instill positive reinforcements, scholastic standards along with a broad exposure to professional career choices. Blue Promise acheives it mission through private donations in addition to corporate and grant funding.

Blue Promise received its 501(c)(3) designation in July 2015. The young men accepted into the mentoring program have no restrictions based upon income, race, or religion.
